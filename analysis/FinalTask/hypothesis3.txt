HYPOTHESIS TEST: AGE VS CONTRACT DURATION
Method: Pearson Correlation Coefficient (r)
Sample Size (n): 32

1. HYPOTHESES:
   H0: Rho = 0 (No association)
   Ha: Rho != 0 (Significant association)

2. DATA SUMMARY:
   Correlation Coefficient (r): 0.591
   95% CI: [0.303, 0.777]

3. CALCULATION:
   t = r * sqrt((n-2) / (1 - r^2))
   t = 0.591 * sqrt(30 / (1 - 0.349))
   t = 4.013
   Degrees of Freedom (df) = 30

4. RESULTS:
   p-value < 0.001
   Significance Level (alpha) = 0.05

5. CONCLUSION:
   Reject H0. There is a significant moderate positive correlation between age and contract duration.
